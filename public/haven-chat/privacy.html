1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, subscription details
  • Chat Messages: Conversations with your EI companion for service improvement
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we don't store card details)
  • Personalization Data: Your companion preferences and interaction history

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address
  • Session Data: Login times, session duration, feature usage

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Provide personalized EI companion experiences
  • Account Management: Process payments, manage subscriptions
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Communication: Send service updates, support responses
  • Safety: Monitor for crisis situations and provide appropriate resources

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these situations:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate (e.g., Stripe for payments)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Crisis Situations: If we believe someone is in immediate danger
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Kept while your account is active
  • Chat History: Stored for service improvement, can be deleted upon request
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by law for tax and accounting purposes

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to provide our services. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your privacy rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Haven is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform.
